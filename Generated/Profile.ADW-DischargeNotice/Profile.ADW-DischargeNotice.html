    The following constrained FHIR profiles are available in this message.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    ADW-Message-Bundle-1BundleA Bundle resource constrained to meet the requirements of ADW messaging.
    ADW-DischargeNotice-MessageHeader-1MessageHeaderThe ADW Discharge Notice Notice constraint is a constrained version of the MessageHeader resource. It is the header for the message exchange which is requesting an action.
    ADW-Discharge-ReferralRequest-1ReferralRequestThe ADW Referral Request constraint is a constrained version of the ReferralRequest resource. It is used to share information to support a referral or transfer of care between practitioners and/or providers.
    CareConnect-ADW-Encounter-1EncounterThe constrained CareConnect ADW encounter resource which represents an encounter between a care professional and the patient.
    CareConnect-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource represents the patient involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    CareConnect-Practitioner-1PractitionerThe Practitioner resource represents the healthcare professional directly or indirectly involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    CareConnect-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents the organisation that employs the healthcare professional.
    CareConnect-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
    ADW-Discharge-QuestionnaireResponse-1QuestionnaireResponseThe ADW Discharge Questionnaire Response constraint is a constrained version of the QuestionnaireResponse resource and carries information about the response to ADW related questions in relation to patient discharge.
    ADW-Condition-1ConditionThe ADW Condition constraint is a constrained version of the Condition resource used to carry the reason for a patient's hospital admission within the context of an ADW Assessment Notice message. It could also be used to record detailed information about conditions, problems or diagnoses recognised by a clinician in other messages across the specification.
